ONE-ON-ONE COUNSELLING FOR TEENAGERS

  • Are you constantly bothered about your looks?
  • Are you finding it difficult to concentrate in your studies?
  • Are you confused about what stream or career to choose?
  • Do you feel people don't listen to you? Or that they don't understand you?
  • Do you feel that people don't care about what you think or feel?
  • Are you confused about any area of your life and wish to understand yourself better?
  • Do you wish you were able to be more kind and friendly towards yourself and like yourself a little more?

Connect with me to work on the following

  • challenges with studies;
  • if you find yourself stuck in a painful conversation with parents, or friends;
  • if you wish to be more comfortable with your appearance;
  • if you wish to explore what to do next
  • or explore anything that matters to you.

WHAT TO EXPECT

  • Each session lasts an hour every week or more depending upon your need.
  • We meet one-on-one in my office in Siliguri, West Bengal or over the phone as you choose.
  • A closer look at what you are experiencing and wanting while I listen to you with love and compassion.
  • I will share deepening practices to help you reflect and get moving faster.
  • Yes, I want to get moving
