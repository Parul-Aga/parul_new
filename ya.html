ONE-ON-ONE COUNSELLING FOR YOUNG ADULTS & ADULTS

Are you looking for a safe space to share what’s going on within you?
  • Do you feel stuck, and find that you are struggling to move- with your goals, at work, with your children or spouse or in a relationship?
  • Are you bursting with things going on and can’t hold on any longer; and longing for a space to just be?
  • Do you wish there was someone you could freely talk to?

WHAT TO EXPECT

  • An hourly session per week or more depending upon your need.
  • We meet one-on-one in my office in Siliguri, West Bengal or over the phone as you choose.
  • In the first session, we begin with identifying the areas you want to work on.
  • In the following sessions, we shall together explore areas you feel stuck in.
  • I shall offer my authentic compassionate listening and reflect to help you hear yourself deeper, and widen your frame of vision.
